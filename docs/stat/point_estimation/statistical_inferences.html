充分統計量（Sufficient Statistics）​

  • Idea: 認為樣本 X~\utilde{X}X​ 包含了所有關於參數 θ\thetaθ 的信息。我們希望使用的統計量能夠保留所有關於參數 θ\thetaθ 的信息。

A stat T=T(X~)T=T(\utilde{X})T=T(X​) is sufficient for θ\thetaθ (or sufficient for F\mathscr{F}F, where F={ f(:θ):θ∈Ω }\mathscr{F}=\set{f(:\theta):\theta\in\Omega}F={f(:θ):θ∈Ω})

iff the conditional distribution of X~\utilde{X}X​ given T(X~)T(\utilde{X})T(X​) does not depend on θ\thetaθ.

i.e. Pθ(X~≤x~∣T=t)⊥θP_\theta(\utilde{X}\le\utilde{x}|T=t)\perp\thetaPθ​(X​≤x​∣T=t)⊥θ

最小充分統計量（Minimal Sufficient Statistics）​

Definition

A sufficient stat T∗T^*T∗ is called minimal sufficient for θ\thetaθ

iff ∀\forall∀ suff stat TTT for θ\thetaθ, ∃\exists∃ function hhh such that T∗=h(T)T^*=h(T)T∗=h(T).

最小充分統計量可以從其他任何充分統計量歸納得到，並且無法進一步歸納（縮減信息）。即信息量在足夠充分的情況下是最小的。

危險

最小統計量不是指統計量的維度最小，而是信息量最小。

Theorem

Let X~∼iidf(x~;θ)\utilde{X}\stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} f(\utilde{x};\theta)X​∼iidf(x​;θ), θ∈Ω\theta\in\Omegaθ∈Ω, suppose ∃a\exist a∃a stat T=T(X~)T=T(\utilde{X})T=T(X​) s.t. f(x~;θ)f(y~;θ)⊥θ  ⟺  T(x~)=T(y~)\frac{f(\utilde{x};\theta)}{f(\utilde{y};\theta)}\perp\theta\iff T(\utilde{x})=T(\utilde{y})f(y​;θ)f(x​;θ)​⊥θ⟺T(x​)=T(y​)

then T=T(X~)T=T(\utilde{X})T=T(X​) is minimal sufficient for θ\thetaθ.
